Swiggy: Delivering Deliciousness to Your Doorstep

Swiggy is an Indian online food ordering and delivery platform. Besides food delivery, the platform also provides on-demand grocery deliveries under the name Instamart, and a same-day package delivery service called Swiggy Genie.

This web-page uses data from Kaggle. The dataset contains valuable information about various aspects of restaurants, enabling users to explore, analyze, and gain insights into the restaurant landscape on Swiggy.


The dataset coontains following attributes:

Column Name Description
ID The ID of the restaurant
Area The area in which the restaurant is located
City The area in which the restaurant is located
Restaurant Name of the restaurant
Price Total bill amount of the order
Ratings Customer rating for the order
Total_Ratings Total ratings people rated
Food type List of food items ordered
Address Address of the Customer
Delivery time Time taken to deliver(in minutes)

The Data page displays one restaurant from each city.
